Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does a dumpster rental cost?

The price depends on the size of the container, the type of debris, and your location. Dumpster rental prices generally include delivery, pickup, rental duration, and a weight limit. Contact us for a precise quote based on your specific needs.

2. What items are prohibited in the dumpster?

You cannot dispose of hazardous materials, including paint, tires, batteries, asbestos, and motor oil. Placing these items in a waste dumpster rental can lead to significant fines and refusal of service at the landfill.

3. How much does it cost to rent a dumpster nationwide?

Dumpster rental prices fluctuate based on the local disposal fees in your state, the size of the container, and the weight of your debris. Generally, costs range from $350 to $850. For a precise, all-inclusive quote for a garbage container rental price in your specific zip code, please contact our dispatch team.

4. How long is the rental period?

Standard rental periods typically range from 7 to 14 days. If you require the dumpster rental for a longer period, we can usually arrange an extension for a small daily fee.

5. Will the dumpster damage my driveway?

Roll-off bins are heavy, especially when full. We recommend placing plywood boards down before the dumpster delivery to protect your driveway from scratches or cracks. Our drivers are trained to place bins carefully.
